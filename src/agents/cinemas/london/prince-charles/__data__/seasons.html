BATMAN DAY

All 3 Films from Christopher Nolan's THE DARK KNIGHT TRILOGY screening in the same day!


This is NOT A MARATHON! Each film is sold separately!


You are welcome to make your own Marathon by buying tickets to each, but you will be asked to leave the building and to rejoin the queue at the end of each film. This will not only allow our staff to safely clean the screens after each film, but to also allow you to get fresh air after a long film. Also customers leaving the screens after each film, means we can turn our Rensair machines in the auditorium on full blast and replace the air in the entire room.
