Cinematic Escapes

December 2020

Lose yourself in the magic of cinema this month as we curate this special programme.

Introduction by Lead Programmer Justin Johnson

Introduction

‘I’ve seen things you people wouldn’t believe. Attack ships on fire off the shoulder of Orion...’
Blade Runner

In these strange times it’s important to allow yourself some escapism: what better place than the cinema? There has never been a more perfect time to lose yourself in the big screen and travel to new worlds, or return to your favourite characters. This December we’ve scheduled some screenings of ever-popular titles – films that we think will lift your spirts, broaden your horizons and offer a fun, shared experience with your loved ones. With something to suit all tastes, our special selection includes an inspiring superhero, an 18th-century romance, a mind-blowing anime, an Italian masterpiece and a scintillating sci-fi classic. So on these cold and dreary winter days, hunker down into your cinema seat and prepare to be whisked away…

Justin Johnson
