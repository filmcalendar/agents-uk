Mank

David Fincher's first feature since 2014's Gone Girl takes us back in time to the making of what is now thought to be the greatest film of all time. The 1930s ‘golden age’ of Hollywood becomes the subject of assessment through the eyes of scathing social critic and alcoholic screenwriter Herman J. Mankiewicz (Gary Oldman). As he races to finish the screenplay of Citizen Kane for Orson Welles (The Souvenir's Tom Burke), he must navigate the pomp and politics of the Dream Factory. Shot in pristine black-and-white, with an inspired performance from Oldman, Fincher's return to the movies has been well worth the wait.

Director:

David Fincher

Starring:

Gary Oldman, Lily Collins, Amanda Seyfried

Running Time:
